What is testing?

Software testing (ST)

A set of activities to discover defects and evaluate the quality of software artifacts.

ISTQB Syllabus v4.0


The process of exercising or evaluating a system or system component by manual or automated means to verify that it satisfies specified requirements.

IEEE 83a


The act of checking whether software satisfies expectations. ST can provide objective, independent information about the quality of software and the risk of its failure to a user or sponsor.
